About Us

B.V.V.S has a strong emphasis on rural development coupled with philanthropic and social commitment. This network of institutions has created an excellent base both administratively and culturally for healthy integrated development of education and training for the social, economic, moral, and spiritual upliftment of society. A variety of formal programs leading to Degrees and Diplomas are offered by these educational institutions. The institutions have been highly acclaimed for providing state-of-the-art education backed by good physical infrastructure and qualified faculty. This has been achieved by integrating the expertise available with the constituent institutions of B.V.V.S.
